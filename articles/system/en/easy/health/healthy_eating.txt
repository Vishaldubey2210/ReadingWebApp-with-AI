Eating healthy food is very important for our bodies. It gives us the energy to run, play, and learn. A good diet should include different types of food like fruits, vegetables, grains, and proteins. Fruits and vegetables are full of vitamins that protect us from getting sick. Drinking plenty of water is also a key part of staying healthy. Try to avoid too much sugar and fast food. When you eat well, you feel good and have more strength throughout the day.
